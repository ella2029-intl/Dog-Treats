Peanut Butter And Oat Recipe

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 25 minutes

Servings: About 20 treats

Our homemade dog treats are inexpensive and completely organic. A simply made recipe thats easy to follow and quick to make and are completely dog-approved.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Rolled Oats
  • ½ cup peanut butter (make sure it does not contain xylitol)
  • 1 ripe banana, mashed
  • 1 egg (optional, for a firmer texture)
  • 1 cup Rolled Oats
  • ½ cup peanut butter (make sure it does not contain xylitol)
  • 1 ripe banana, mashed
  • 1 egg (optional, for a firmer texture)

Materials

  • Mixing bowl
  • Spoon or spatula
  • Fork or potato masher (for banana)
  • Mesauring cups
  • Baking sheet
  • Parchment paper
  • Cooling rack

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C)
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper
  3. In the mixing bowl, mash the banana until smooth
  4. Add the peanut butter and stir till combined
  5. Mix in the oats (and egg if using) until dough forms
  6. Roll dough into small balls and flaten with fork to make biscuits
  7. Arrange he treats on the pre-prepared baking sheet
  8. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes, or until lightly golden
  9. Cool completely on cooling rack before giving to your pup
